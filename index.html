GamePad Support

This site is built with support for the GamePad API! Everything is usable with just the gamepad, borrowing some ideas from how touch support was built for the iPad, with an adaptable cursor.

If you have a gamepad connected your your computer, just press any button to get started!

You can use the Left Stick to move the cursor, and the Right Stick to scroll on the page. Use the Action Button to click!
